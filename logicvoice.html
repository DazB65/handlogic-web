How LogicVoice works

Press. Speak. Done.

No wake words, no cloud assistant listening all day. It hears you only when you ask it to.

1

Hit your hotkey and speak

A small pill appears while it listens. Say the thing — a command for your Mac, in plain words.

2

See exactly what will run

AI turns your words into AppleScript. You see the script, and anything risky asks for confirmation first. Common commands you've saved as macros skip the AI entirely and run instantly.

3

Or just dictate

A second hotkey starts dictation mode: speak, and your words are typed into whatever app you're in — with optional AI cleanup. Press Escape to cancel anytime.

Features

A power tool, not a Siri clone.

Built for the Raycast / Alfred / Keyboard Maestro crowd — people who want control, not magic.

🗣️

Speak commands

Your words become AppleScript, generated by AI and run on the spot. The script is shown to you, and risky actions always ask first.

⚡

Instant macros

Save your common commands as macros — they run deterministically, instantly, with no AI call and no tokens spent.

✍️

Dictation mode

A dedicated hotkey for pure dictation: your speech is pasted literally into any app, with optional AI cleanup. Escape cancels.

💊

A HUD that stays out of the way

A compact floating pill — a mic orb and a label. It never steals focus from what you're doing.

🔑

Your keys, your models

Bring your own OpenAI API key and pick your model. No subscription, no middleman marking up your AI calls.

🧮

See what it costs

A built-in cost counter shows exactly what every command costs, down to the token. Most sessions run on pennies.
