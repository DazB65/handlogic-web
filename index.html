The approach

An indie dev, a Mac, and one quiet idea.

HandLogic is the studio behind the kind of Mac apps I wanted to use myself — small, fast, native and quiet. No chat sidebar that won't go away. No agentic monster trying to run my life. Just sharp tools that share one window, the way Mac apps used to feel.

The Mac apps are built native-first with SwiftUI — native materials, your system theme, fast launch, almost no memory. Your data stays yours across every app: bring your own AI keys, no analytics SDKs, no telemetry, no ads. Shipped from Brisbane, Australia.
